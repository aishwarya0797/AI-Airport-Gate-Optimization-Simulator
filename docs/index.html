What it actually does

Six stages, one pipeline — generate a schedule, allocate and optimize it, then verify, predict, and export.

✈

Synthetic flight generation

Realistic schedules across airlines, aircraft sizes, and weather scenarios — weighted to your actual gate capacity.

⚙

OR-Tools optimization

Minimizes walking distance and maximizes seated flights, with a bounded solve time so it never hangs.

⚠

Conflict detection

Flags gate overlaps, size mismatches, and double-bookings before they become a real problem.

🧠

ML predictions + XAI

Delay risk, gate recommendations, and conflict probability — each with a plain-language explanation.

▶

Live animated simulation

Watch aircraft move onto, sit at, and push back from gates across a full simulated day.

▤

Exportable reports

CSV and PDF exports for every stage — schedules, conflicts, utilization, and optimization results.
